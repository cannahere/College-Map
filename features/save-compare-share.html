Save, Edit, Compare & Share Colleges

Organize your college list in one place — save schools, edit as you explore, compare options, print clean lists, and share the exact same view.

Building a college list shouldn't mean juggling multiple tabs, scattered notes, or losing track of schools as you explore. Save, Compare, Print & Share keeps everything organized in one place.

Save colleges as you discover them, edit your list as it grows, compare key details side by side, print a clean version for meetings or planning, and share the exact same view with family members or counselors—all without creating an account.

Why organization matters

College exploration is a process, not a one-time decision. Your list will grow, change, and evolve as you learn more. Save, Compare, Print & Share helps you:

  • Keep all your options in one place instead of scattered across tabs
  • Compare key details side by side to make informed decisions
  • Edit your list as you explore without losing previous work
  • Print clean lists for meetings with counselors or family discussions
  • Share the exact same view with others so everyone sees the same information

How to use Save, Compare, Print & Share

  1. Tap any college on the map and press "Save" to add it to your list.
  2. Open "My Saved List" to see all your saved colleges.
  3. Edit your list by removing schools that no longer interest you.
  4. Use the comparison table to see key details side by side.
  5. Print a clean version of your list for meetings or planning.
  6. Share your list with family or counselors—they'll see the exact same view.

Why this solves real organization pain

  • You don't lose track of schools in multiple tabs or notes.
  • You can compare options side by side instead of switching between pages.
  • You can edit your list as you explore without starting over.
  • You can print clean lists for meetings and planning.
  • You can share the exact same view with others for better collaboration.

Save, Compare, Print & Share FAQ

Can I edit my college list after saving schools?

Yes. You can update, refine, and reorganize your list as you explore more colleges.

Can I compare multiple colleges at once?

Yes. The comparison table shows key info side by side so you can make informed decisions faster.

What does "share your list" mean?

Sharing generates a link that shows others exactly what you see — your saved schools plotted on the map.

Do I need to create an account to save colleges?

No. Saving and comparing works without login during your session.
